Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

This is a place where we teachers and students pray. On the whole campus of St. Xavier's School, this is one of the most important places built.


School Library

Was built in 1980

Students come to the school library to check out books, study, or to stay in a quiet area for a while. We built the library mainly for the education of our students. There are a large collection of books from many genres.


School Auditorium

Was built in 1982

The school auditorium consists of many seats and a large stage. We host many shows and plays on this part of the campus. The main reason that we built the auditorium was for the kids to have some fun along with their education and learning.
